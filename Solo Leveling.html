[i] System Rules

Global XP & Subquests

Standard quests grant XP based on frequency: Daily (10 XP), Weekly (70 XP), Monthly (300 XP). If a quest has Subquests (a checklist), each tick grants customized XP, but the main quest is only considered "Completed" for your stat graph when all subquests are checked.

Dashboards & Charts

Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and Negative quests are isolated into sub-tabs. The Radar chart in each tab only calculates completion percentages using quests of that exact frequency and type.

Negative Quests

Negative quests represent bad habits. Ticking them means you failed, removing XP. Leaving them unticked means you successfully resisted, granting you positive XP and preserving stat consistency.

Journal & Ranking

You can add specific notes to any quest log using the [💬] icon. Ranks are calculated based on the Previous Week's total consistency across all active frequencies and types combined.
