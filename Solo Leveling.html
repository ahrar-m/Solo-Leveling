[i] System Progression Rules

Quest Proficiency (Leveling)

Every time you complete a specific daily quest, that quest individually gains 10 XP.

Leveling up a quest requires an increasing amount of XP based on its current level:

  • Level 1 ➝ 2 requires 10 XP (1 Completion)
  • Level 2 ➝ 3 requires 20 XP (2 Completions)
  • Level 3 ➝ 4 requires 30 XP (3 Completions)... etc.

Stat Weekly Rankings

Each Core Stat receives a Rank based on your average quest completion percentage. The cycle resets every Sunday.

At the start of the week, your percentage naturally starts at 0% and climbs as you tick off quests.

[ A-Rank ]
81% - 100%
[ B-Rank ]
61% - 80%
[ C-Rank ]
41% - 60%
[ D-Rank ]
21% - 40%
[ E-Rank ]
0% - 20%

The S-Rank Awakening

To achieve the legendary [ S-Rank ] status for a specific stat, you must prove absolute consistency.

Requirement: Maintain an A-Rank (81%+) for the *current* week, while also having achieved an A-Rank in the *previous* week.
